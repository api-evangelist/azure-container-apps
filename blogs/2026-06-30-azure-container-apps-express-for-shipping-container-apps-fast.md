Introduces Azure Container Apps Express, a streamlined deployment model using MicroVM-based startup and memory/disk state restoration to achieve roughly 1.5 second cold starts versus about 20 seconds for standard Consumption environments. First-time deployment time drops from roughly 166 seconds to 52 seconds, aimed at startups and product teams needing predictable wake-up behavior with minimal infrastructure overhead.
