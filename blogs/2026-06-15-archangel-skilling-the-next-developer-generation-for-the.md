AI is transforming the SDLC at speed but there's a quieter question following close behind. If the code is being written for your junior developers, when do they learn the skills to become senior ones and how can they use these tools without losing understanding of what they've made? Most tools try to solve this by catching issues after the commit, but by then the teaching moment is already gone.
